About Oliver

  • Age: 7 years old
  • Breed: Labrador & Pyr mix
  • Neutered: Yes

Food and Water

Oliver is used to eating at 5pm. Sometimes he forgets to drink water. He may need to be reminded.

Treats

Oliver is food motivated! Treats are a great way to let him know he’s doing well. They can also be helpful when he’s feeling a bit anxious.

Home Alone

Oliver can get scared when left alone. His safe space is his bed. Before I leave the house I ask him to sit in his bed and lay down. I talk to him and tell him he is going to stay. He understands this. A treat always helps calm his nerves.

Walks and Exercise

Oliver is happiest when he gets at least 2 walks a day. At home he walks every day without a leash on a wooded trail. He should be leashed near a road or walking through a neighborhood (so many smells!)

Bedtime

Oliver prefers to sleep near someone at night, but with a little encouragement he will be OK sleeping on his own in another room.

Interactions

Oliver is a friendly and curious boy who is always excited to meet and smell new people.
